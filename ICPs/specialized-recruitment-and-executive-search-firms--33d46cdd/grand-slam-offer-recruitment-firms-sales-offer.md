**Parent ICP:** [[ICPs/specialized-recruitment-and-executive-search-firms--33d46cdd|Specialized recruitment and executive search firms]]

_Filled for a business serving specialized recruitment and executive search firms with sales pipeline generation, qualification, and meeting booking. This does not cover delivery of recruiting/search work itself._

## Assumptions

**These are explicit assumptions used to fill the template before live prospect validation.**

1. The target client is a boutique to mid-sized specialized recruitment or executive search firm with 5-50 employees.
2. The buyer is usually the founder, managing partner, commercial lead, or head of business development.
3. They already know how to deliver recruiting/search work, but they do **not** have a predictable new-business engine.
4. Their average placement / search fee is high enough that one extra signed search can materially justify a premium sales offer.
5. Their main commercial bottleneck is not awareness of their niche, but inconsistent prospecting, weak qualification, and poor meeting conversion.
6. They want more meetings with hiring managers, talent leaders, founders, and business unit leaders inside target accounts.
7. They are open to outbound, referral activation, LinkedIn, email, list-building, qualification frameworks, and sales process changes.
8. They do **not** want a generic lead-gen agency; they want a niche-specific commercial system for selling retained search / specialist recruitment services.
9. They value speed to first meetings more than perfect automation.
10. The best initial delivery model is a hybrid: strategy + build + done-with-you execution + light done-for-you setup.
11. They are willing to pay for revenue outcomes, but they are skeptical because many agencies promise meetings and deliver junk leads.
12. Any pricing, competitor range, proof points, and conversion metrics below are **assumption-based placeholders** until validated with real buyers and pilot data.

**Validation note:** All unchecked boxes below are intentionally left unchecked where live customer evidence is still required.

## 6 Bundle the Grand-Slam Offer

**Problem:** They target the wrong accounts and waste effort on bad-fit prospects.

→ Solution: How to focus only on buyers with real hiring potential.

→ **Search-Mandate Target Map** (CHF 2,500)

- ICP definition workshop for niche, function, geography, and search type
- Buyer map across founder, CEO, business leader, talent lead, and HR owner
- 100-account target list with trigger-event filters
- Priority tiers and outreach sequencing rules

**Problem:** Their messaging sounds generic and doesn't earn replies.

→ Solution: How to sound commercially sharp, niche-specific, and worth meeting.

→ **Authority Messaging Engine** (CHF 2,000)

- Positioning statement for specialized recruitment / executive search buyers
- 3 outbound angles tied to trigger events and hiring pain
- Email, LinkedIn, and voicemail scripts
- Objection and proof-point library

**Problem:** They get calls, but too many never become real opportunities.

→ Solution: How to qualify hard before the calendar fills with weak meetings.

→ **Qualified-Meeting Filter System** (CHF 2,500)

- Qualification scorecard for budget, urgency, role criticality, and hiring ownership
- Red-flag disqualification checklist
- Discovery call flow and note-taking template
- Calendar booking rules and pre-call questionnaire

**Problem:** They fail to follow up consistently and lose warm opportunities.

→ Solution: How to stay in the deal without sounding spammy.

→ **No-Drop Follow-Up Machine** (CHF 1,800)

- 21-day and 45-day follow-up sequences
- Multi-channel touch plan across email, LinkedIn, and call tasks
- CRM reminder logic and next-step rules
- Recap and reactivation templates

**Problem:** First meetings do not reliably turn into signed search mandates.

→ Solution: How to move from conversation to scoped commercial opportunity.

→ **Mandate Conversion Kit** (CHF 3,000)

- First-meeting agenda and next-step structure
- Opportunity recap template
- Proposal / retained search offer template
- Stakeholder mapping and second-meeting booking script

**Problem:** The founder is still the sales engine.

→ Solution: How to make the pipeline run beyond one rainmaker.

→ **Commercial Cadence Playbook** (CHF 2,200)

- Weekly pipeline review agenda
- Role ownership across founder, recruiter, BD, and ops
- KPI dashboard for meetings, qualification rate, second-meeting rate, and mandate rate
- 30-day adoption plan for the team

### Grand-Slam offer name

**Core promise:** Build and install the commercial system that helps specialized recruitment and executive search firms book more qualified buyer meetings, qualify harder, and convert more first conversations into signed search opportunities.
